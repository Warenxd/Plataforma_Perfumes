Polo Blue by Ralph Lauren is a Aromatic Fougere fragrance for men. Polo Blue was launched in 2003. Polo Blue was created by Carlos Benaïm and Christophe Laudamiel. Top notes are Cucumber, Melon and Mandarin Orange; middle notes are Basil, Sage and Geranium; base notes are Suede, Woodsy Notes and Musk.
